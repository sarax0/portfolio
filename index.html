Network Log & Alarm Intelligence — SysLogs

Multi-component ML project for network fault detection and root-cause analysis, processing millions of log messages ingested from Elasticsearch across network devices scaled from 5 to 12.

  • Log anomaly detection (LogBERT): adapted the open-source LogBERT transformer to telecom log data, developing the full preprocessing pipeline from scratch (Drain3 template parsing into sequential model inputs); introduced PEFT fine-tuning for incremental learning, cutting retraining time on millions of logs.
  • Novel log detection (first-time-seen): flags log messages outside the model's training distribution to identify novel fault types early.
  • Log–alarm association & co-occurrence: predicts expected logs from alarm occurrences and identifies causal log–alarm relationships within time windows, shortening root-cause identification.
PyTorchTransformersPEFT Drain3ElasticsearchAnomaly Detection
